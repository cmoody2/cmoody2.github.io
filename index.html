About Me

Born and raised in Las Vegas, I started my working career in hospitality.
While growing up, I spent much of my time fiddling with computers, video game consoles and the like but I never ventured into the software behind them.

In 2019 I had finally found my introduction into software development through the editing of .hex files from a github repo for mechanical keyboards and
I've been hooked ever since.

I am a graduate of The Tech Academy's Software Developer Boot Camp.

In my free time you can usually find me researching or building
custom mechanical keyboards.
I also enjoy indoor bouldering, cycling and pc gaming.
